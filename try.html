About Tub ‘n Cup

Tub N Cup is a fusion of pet grooming and cafe. It is an oasis for Pets to be groomed and bathed in a luxury tub while Pawrents get refreshed with a cup of drink from our cafe. While Tub N Cup is a pet cafe, our customers are not limited to pets and their pawrents. A selection of innovative and delectable of Milkteas, Fraps, and Lattes as well as currently trendy savory treats like the Cream Cheese Buns, Biscoff Donuts, Fries, and Pizzas are available for anyone to treat themselves with either at the IG-worthy spots inside the shop or for take aways.

Pet Owners and Non-Pet Owners are welcome!
